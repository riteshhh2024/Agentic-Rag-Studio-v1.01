Acme Corp Customer Support Policy

1. Refund Policy
Customers may request a full refund within 30 days of purchase. Refunds are processed
within 5-7 business days. To initiate a refund, contact support@acme.com with your
order number and reason for return.

2. Escalation Procedure
If a customer issue cannot be resolved within 24 hours, the support agent must escalate
the ticket to the L2 team using the escalation form in the internal portal. The L2 team
will respond within 4 business hours.

3. Warranty Claims
All hardware products come with a 1-year limited warranty. To file a warranty claim,
customers must provide proof of purchase and a description of the defect. Approved claims
receive a replacement unit within 10 business days.

4. Data Privacy
Acme Corp is committed to protecting customer data. All personal information is stored
encrypted and never shared with third parties without explicit consent. Customers may
request data deletion at any time by contacting privacy@acme.com.

5. Service Level Agreements
Enterprise customers have guaranteed response times of 2 hours for critical issues and
8 hours for standard issues. SLA violations trigger automatic escalation to the account
manager and a service credit of 10% of the monthly fee.
