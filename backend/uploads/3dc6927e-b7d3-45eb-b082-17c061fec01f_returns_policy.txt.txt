 Acme Returns and Warranty Policy

  Standard Returns:
  Customers may return any item within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
  Items must be in original condition with proof of purchase.

  Extended Returns for Damaged Goods:
  If an item arrives damaged, customers have 60 days to file a claim.
  Damaged item returns require photo evidence submitted via support@acme.com.

  Warranty Coverage:
  All hardware products include a 12-month limited warranty.
  Warranty claims are processed within 10 business days.
  Replacement units are shipped at no cost to the customer.

  Refund Processing:
  Standard refunds are processed in 5-7 business days.
  Express refund (24h) is available for orders over $200.
  Refunds are issued to the original payment method only.
