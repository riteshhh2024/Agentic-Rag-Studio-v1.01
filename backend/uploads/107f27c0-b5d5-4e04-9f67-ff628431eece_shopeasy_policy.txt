  SHOPEASY RETURNS & REFUNDS POLICY

  RETURN WINDOW
  Customers may return any item within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused, in original packaging, with tags attached. Electronics have a 15-day return window due to manufacturer requirements.

  HOW TO INITIATE A RETURN
  1. Log into your ShopEasy account and go to Orders.
  2. Select the order and click "Request Return".
  3. Choose the reason: Damaged, Wrong Item, Changed Mind, or Other.
  4. Print the prepaid return label and drop the package at any courier point.
  5. Refund is processed within 5–7 business days of the package arriving at our warehouse.

  REFUND METHODS
  - Original payment method: Full refund within 5–7 business days.
  - ShopEasy wallet credit: Instant credit, 10% bonus added.
  - Exchange: No shipping fee on the replacement item.

  NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS
  The following cannot be returned: Perishables, personalized/custom orders, gift cards, intimate apparel, and hazardous materials.

  DAMAGED OR WRONG ITEMS
  If you receive a damaged or wrong item, contact support@shopeasy.com within 48 hours of delivery with photos. We will arrange a free return and send a replacement or full refund — no questions asked.

  SHIPPING POLICY
  Standard shipping: 3–5 business days, free on orders above $50.
  Express shipping: 1–2 business days, $9.99 flat.
  Same-day delivery: Available in select cities, $14.99.
  International shipping: 7–14 business days, rates calculated at checkout.

  CANCELLATIONS
  Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement for a full refund. After 2 hours, the order enters fulfilment and cannot be cancelled.
